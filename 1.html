What Is Nexathon?

Nexathon is an intensive data science and machine learning competition where innovators, developers, and data enthusiasts collaborate to solve challenging real-world problems.

It's a platform to learn, build, and showcase your skills in a competitive yet supportive environment.

  • Focus: Data Science, Machine Learning, and AI.
  • Goal: Create an impact by implementing data-driven solutions.
  • Datasets: Work with unique, large-scale datasets provided by our partners.
