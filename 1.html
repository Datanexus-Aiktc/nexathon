What Is Nexathon?

Nexathon is an intensive data science and machine learning competition where innovators, developers, and data enthusiasts collaborate to solve challenging real-world problems.

It's a platform to learn, build, and showcase your skills in a competitive yet supportive environment.

  • Focus: Data Science, Machine Learning, and AI.
  • Goal: Create an impact by implementing data-driven solutions.
  • Datasets: Work with unique, large-scale datasets provided by our partners.

Nexathon Objectives

  • 1.Enhance Digital Skills Proficiency
  • 2.Foster Innovation and Creativity
  • 3.Promote Interdisciplinary Collaboration
  • 4.Achieve National Recognition
  • 5.Enhance Employability
  • 6.Develop Real-World Problem-Solving Skills

Nexathon Outcomes

  • 1. Participants will demonstrate proficiency in digital skills through practical application and hands-on learning.
  • 2. Participants will showcase innovative and creative thinking in their web design solutions.
  • 3. The event will cultivate a collaborative environment, fostering communication, teamwork, and problem-solving abilities.
