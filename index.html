What is Nexathon?

Nexathon is an intensive data science and machine learning competition where innovators, developers, and data enthusiasts collaborate to solve challenging real-world problems. It's a platform to learn, build, and showcase your skills in a competitive yet supportive environment.

  • Focus: Data Science, Machine Learning, and AI.
  • Goal: Create an impact by impementing data-driven solutions.
  • Datasets: Work with unique, large-scale datasets provided by our partners.

Guidelines

  1. Teams must have 2 to 4 members. Solo participation is not allowed.
  2. Your project must be related to Data Science technologies such as Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Power BI, R, or similar fields.
  3. Your Project must be completed before the deadline.
  4. Submit your project as a link to a public Git repository (GitHub, GitLab, etc.).
  5. Judging criteria: Technical Accuracy, Creativity, Scalability, and Clarity of Presentation.
  6. Any misappropriate behavior will lead to immediate disqualification.

Rules of Nexathon

  • All participants must follow the event’s Code of Conduct to ensure a safe and respectful environment.
  • All intellectual property created during the hackathon belongs to the participants.
  • You should not exceed the time of your presentation.
  • Teams must be present at the venue for the entire duration of the hackathon.
  • I agree that the registration fee is non-refundable, even if I am not selected for the final round.
